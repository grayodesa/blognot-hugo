Фальшивый пейзаж Ван Гога оказался подлинным

Прекрасная история о том, как неоднократно объявленная подделкой картина Ван Гога оказалась подлинником:

The work, titled «Sunset at Montmajour,» depicts a landscape dominated by trees in various shades of green. It is believed to have been painted during the time the Dutch artist spent in Arles, in the south of France, in 1888 and 1889.

The declaration of authenticity was made Monday by the Van Gogh Museum in Amsterdam, which itself had declared the canvas a fake less than two decades ago.

«A discovery of this magnitude has never before occurred in the history of the Van Gogh Museum,» the museum’s director, Axel Rüger, said in a statement.

«But what makes this even more exceptional is that this is a transition work in his oeuvre, and moreover, a large painting from a period that is considered by many to be the culmination of his artistic achievement.»

Museum officials didn’t estimate the value of the painting, but Van Gogh’s works have sold for more than $50 million.

The current owner asked to remain anonymous.Wall Street Journal
