Беженцы из России возглавляют мировой

Беженцы из России возглавляют мировой список ищущих убежища в развитых странах — так утверждается в докладе Верховного Комиссара ООН по делам беженцев.

«Russians, mostly Chechens, were easily the top asylum-seeking nationality in 2003,» UNHCR spokesman Rupert Colville told reporters Tuesday.

The agency said 33,364 Russians requested asylum in industrialized countries, primarily in eastern and western Europe — 68 percent higher than the 19,904 the previous year.
Applicants for asylum from Serbia and Montenegro were in second place, totaling 24,753, down from 32,078 in 2002. In third place were Iraqis, with 24,744 seeking asylum, half the number of the previous year.
Afghans dropped 46 percent to 13,820, putting them in sixth place — behind Turks at 22,912 and Chinese at 21,259.
