> OSLO, Aug 8 (Reuters)&nbsp;&mdash; Online advertising and browser firm Opera Sofware is&nbsp;considering a&nbsp;sale of&nbsp;the company after missing <nobr>second-quarter</nobr> revenue forecasts and cutting its <nobr>full-year</nobr> expectations, the board of&nbsp;the Norwegian company said on&nbsp;Saturday.  
> The search for a&nbsp;buyer, or&nbsp;other forms of&nbsp;partnership, comes &laquo;in&nbsp;response to&nbsp;strategic interest in&nbsp;the company from a&nbsp;number of&nbsp;parties&raquo;, and will be&nbsp;aided by&nbsp;bankers at&nbsp;Morgan Stanley International and ABG Sundal Collier, it&nbsp;added in&nbsp;a&nbsp;statement.<small>&laquo;Reuters&raquo;</small>
